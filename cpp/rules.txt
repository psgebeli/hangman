Rules for the hangman game (this will be the same in all languages)

1. player will guess one letter at a time
2. no proper nouns (names, places, etc) will be used
3. each incorrect guess --> one part of stick figure drawn
   this means the player gets 9 guesses, after which the player
   fails. the order of appearance is :

   a) platform
   b) noose
   c) head
   d) torso
   e) groin
   f) arm 1
   g) arm 2
   h) leg 1
   i) leg 2

4. only lowercase letters can be inputted
